Experiment 2 - Living Impressions

Living Impression

Description

This project offers a generative, living impression of this inspiring image:

Technical

Above is a picture of Mt. Fuji in Japan. The background consists of a mountain range along with a river and a tree in the foreground.

Relation to inspring image:

  • Color: The sky, grass, tree, and stone colors were sampled from the inspiring image using Paint 3D eyedropper tool.
  • Primitives: The trees are rendered with rectangle primitives as the trunks andcircle/ellipsis for the leaves. Additionally, clouds were generated with ellipsis.
  • Polygons: The background mountain is a single randomly generated polygon using vertex calls. The river makes use of two Bezier Vertexes(one for the river, and one for the land).
  • Composition: Different random seed values lead to differently shaped mountains, differently positioned trees, differently shaped rivers, and varied clouds.
  • Life: Slow and meandering clouds with varying speeds and sizes simulate the passage of time. Also, falling cherry blossoms from a tree hint at the presence of gravity.
