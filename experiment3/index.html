Alternate Worlds

Description

Above is an example of autotile. This technique is used to generate maps using symbols and tools.

On the left: This is a map of a plains biome in combination with water (be it lakes, rivers, or oceans). Notice the texured grass, the random placement of houses, trees, and rocks.

On the right: The "?" represents land and the "O" represents water. Feel free to edit the text of the right and see it reflected on the left!
